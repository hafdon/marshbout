      While Fez and Flick sunk into a quiet sloom[1], Carlos, Drav, Paros, and Joan tied up a few loose ends. Carlos helpfully speculated that "maybe Juniper came from a [meat-person] that wore the mask, and Juniper laid a [meat-egg], and then Juniper was inside the meat-egg." Further [afrayning]prompted Ethel to pontificate on the "ancient ways of the three_school" and Juniper's particular interest in the mysteries of life and death. All matters resolved, the gang turned their attentions to [••]; Drav determined that the blurry symbols decorating ••'s shell were lexemes in [an old language used by artificers], and Paros unintentionally presented the tremulous box with visions of evisceration. Before much else could go wrong, [/], a construct isometric to ••, hailed the group and led them to red_fox_university. Passing through a cozy common room inhabited by briffa and reginald, two teenful[2] students, and a sleepy [xorn] named Turkey, where they learned that •• had been running away from an emotionally volatile love triangle with [-] dash and [—] emdash (siblings, no less!). Joan smashed down some doors to track the still fleeing / who brought them ultimately to Sik's [alchemical workshop], where •• received some much-needed relaxation and reprogramming. After smoking [a bit of laced leaf], Sik showed Joan how to turn her [multicolored stone] into a floating force field and pointed them towards Professor_Henriette_Erman who, aside from studying for a license to practice psychiatric medicine, schooled them on the ancient mountain deity [Karnish] and the primordial provenance of the various Three Schools which roam the land. Finally, they were pointed to Ymenestra, a visiting lecturer with a personal and professional interest in depossessions and exorcisms.
